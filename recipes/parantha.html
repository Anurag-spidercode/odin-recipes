Parantha Recipe

Home

Description

Paratha is a flaky, golden-brown Indian flatbread made by layering and cooking dough on a griddle.
Soft inside yet slightly crisp outside, it can be enjoyed plain or stuffed with spiced fillings like potatoes,
paneer, or vegetables. Traditionally cooked with ghee or oil, parathas are a staple of North Indian breakfasts
and are often served hot with yogurt, pickles, or curries.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Potato
  • Onion
  • Masala
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Boil The potato
  2. Mash the boiled potato and add masala and chopped onions
  3. Make dough from flour
  4. Fill the dough with mashed potato mixture
  5. Cook on a tawa and apply some ghee both sides.
  6. Serve with dahi, ghee, pickel
